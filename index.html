About Milena Design Ltd

Quality, Experience and Reliable Manufacturing Since 2015

Milena Design Ltd is a small, family-run manufacturing company established in 2015. Our production facility is based in Greater Manchester, United Kingdom.

We specialise in the manufacture of high-quality garments and knitted textile products. Every item we produce is crafted with exceptional care and attention to detail, ensuring the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

We use only premium-quality materials sourced from trusted UK suppliers. Alongside our own product designs, we also manufacture bespoke products based on our customers' designs, specifications and technical requirements.

Our main expertise is in the production of baby and children's clothing, although we also manufacture a wide range of other textile products.

We work exclusively with business clients, including clothing brands, manufacturers, wholesalers and distributors. We do not accept individual or retail orders.

We are proud to work with companies across the United Kingdom, providing reliable manufacturing, consistent quality and on-time delivery.

Useful information Frequently Asked Questions Manufacturing, sampling and production information for brands and business customers. View FAQ
Do you offer private label clothing manufacturing?

Yes. Milena Design Ltd provides private label clothing manufacturing for fashion brands, retailers, wholesalers and business customers across the UK. We manufacture garments to customer designs, specifications and branding requirements.

Can you manufacture baby clothing and nursery textile products?

Yes. We specialise in baby clothing manufacturing and can also produce nursery textile products, knitted babywear, blankets, soft furnishings and related textile items for business customers.

Do you manufacture children's clothing?

Yes. We manufacture children's clothing, knitted garments, dresses, jumpers, accessories and other textile products according to supplied designs and specifications.

Do you provide sampling and prototype development?

Yes. Our sampling service supports fit testing, design approval, construction checks and production planning before full manufacturing begins.

Do you offer Cut, Make and Trim (CMT) services?

Yes. We provide Cut, Make and Trim (CMT) manufacturing services for brands and businesses that require reliable garment production in the UK.

Can you manufacture bespoke textile products?

Yes. We offer bespoke textile manufacturing based on customer designs, technical specifications, fabrics and production requirements.

Do you work with start-up clothing brands?

Yes. We support established businesses and start-up clothing brands with sampling, small production runs, private label manufacturing and ongoing garment production.

Where is Milena Design Ltd located?

Milena Design Ltd is based in Ashton-under-Lyne, Tameside, Greater Manchester. We provide UK garment manufacturing, textile production and contract sewing services for business customers.

Do you manufacture knitted textile products?

Yes. We manufacture knitted textile products, knitwear, babywear, children's garments, blankets and selected soft furnishing products.

Who do you work with?

We work exclusively with business customers, including clothing brands, manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers and distributors. We do not provide retail or individual made-to-measure services.
